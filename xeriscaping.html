Xeriscaping & Low Water Landscaping

At AZ Desert Designer, we create stunning, sustainable outdoor spaces that thrive in the Arizona desert. Our xeriscaping and low water landscaping designs blend natural beauty with smart water conservation, giving you a landscape that’s both beautiful and practical. Let us transform your yard into a desert oasis that saves water and stands out year-round.

Xeriscaping & Low Water Landscaping Services

Desert Plant Design

We create customized planting designs featuring native and drought tolerant species that thrive in Arizona’s climate. Each design balances color, texture, and sustainability for year round beauty.

Rockscapes & Hardscapes

Enhance your outdoor space with natural stone, gravel, and creative hardscape elements. From walkways to decorative rock gardens, our designs bring structure and style to desert living.

Water Efficient Design

We plan every detail of your landscape with water conservation in mind, from plant placement to soil grading and rock selection. Our designs minimize waste while maintaining a natural desert look.
